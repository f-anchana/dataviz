What
is ...
G-rap

?

G-Rap is a website dedicated to tracking the winners of the best rap song of the year title, compared to the number of streams on Spotify. This website presents a ranking of the most popular rap songs, highlighting the artists and tracks that have managed to captivate a large audience and generate a large number of plays. G-Rap offers rap music fans an overview of current trends and emerging artists dominating the music scene, while highlighting the exceptional performances that have led to these awards.
